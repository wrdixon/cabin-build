Roof Assembly In Progress

Cathedral assembly — no attic space — vented cold roof above continuous insulation

Overview

The roof is a cathedral assembly with no attic space. The design prioritizes a continuous air barrier, exterior insulation to keep the sheathing warm, and a vented cold roof above the insulation layer. ZIP sheathing is already installed and taped; remaining work is polyiso, strapping, and metal panels.

Decisions

1. Layer order (inside to outside)

2x10 rafters at 24" OC with 3.5" Rockwool between (will not fill full rafter depth — air gap retained above insulation before ZIP). ZIP sheathing taped at all seams. Ice and water shield over full ZIP deck. 2" polyiso taped at seams. Vertical strapping (1x4 or similar) fastened with Headlok screws at 24" OC driven into rafters. Standing seam metal panel on strapping.

Reason: keeps the ZIP sheathing above the dew point, provides a vented cold roof cavity above the polyiso, and gives a drainage/drying plane under the metal.

2. Venting strategy

Cor-A-Vent bug blocking at eave, venting ridge cap at peak.

Reason: the strapping cavity needs to breathe at both ends to function as a cold roof. Bug blocking keeps insects out of the cavity without restricting airflow.

3. Panel finish

24-gauge striated or matte finish standing seam panels recommended.

Reason: strapping is at 24" OC driven by rafter layout. That spacing increases oil canning risk on flat panels. Striated or matte finish masks this.

4. Fastening strapping

Headlok screws at 24" OC driven through polyiso into rafters.

Reason: structural screw required to pull through foam and engage rafter. Standard screws insufficient at this foam thickness.

Installation Notes

  1. ZIP sheathing is installed. Confirm all seams are taped before proceeding.
  2. Install Rockwool batts between rafters. Friction fit, no gaps at edges. Retain air space above batts — do not pack to ZIP.
  3. Install ice and water shield over full ZIP deck. Verify compatibility with polyiso facing before install.
  4. Install 2" polyiso over ice and water shield. Tape all seams. Stagger joints if using multiple layers.
  5. Install vertical strapping at 24" OC aligned to rafters. Use Headlok screws, minimum penetration into rafter per Headlok spec. This creates the vented cold roof cavity.
  6. Install Cor-A-Vent at eave before strapping is complete to block insects at cavity entry.
  7. Install standing seam metal panels on strapping. Confirm panel system is rated to span 24" without additional decking.
  8. Install venting ridge cap at peak.
  9. Chimney penetration to be detailed separately once location is confirmed. Do not finalize panel layout at ridge until chimney location is set.
